“载歌在谷”春节晚会，承袭于两届Google谷歌华人春晚的传统，是由硅谷各大高科技公司员工共同参办的湾区千人规模的春节联欢晚会。

The 2017 Bay Area “ZaiGeZaiGu” Chinese New Year Gala continues the tradition, established in 2014, of holding a yearly Chinese New Year celebration at Google. The event will be organized and held by employees of various Silicon Valley tech companies, and will involve over one thousand people. “ZaiGeZaiGu” is Chinese for “Singing in the Valley.”
